Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is The Toshie Club?

The Toshies Club is your entry into the Toshie ecosystem. The adventure begins here. To learn more please read the story (and link “the story” to the main webpage )

2. What are the benefits of The Toshie Club collection?

The team will specify more about it.

3. How are the 10.000 Toshies distributed?

· 2,000: Genesis Collection Members (each member will receive as much NFTs as NFTs holding from the Genesis Collection)
· 250: Airdrop for VIPs
· 250: HASBULLA wallet for future collaborations, marketing, and advisors
· 7500: For the public to mint
· There will be a Public sale if there is not a sell out in Whitelist Minting

4. What’s the Minting price?

Whitelist Mint Price will be 0 ETH, all you have to pay for is the gas price.

5. What are secondary royalties and how will they be used?

The secondary royalty is set to 7.5%. Funds will be used to continue scaling the team in order to deliver projects and long term value to the Toshie community.

6. Will Toshie be revealed right away?

5 days after the mint, you’ll know what your new Toshie looks like!
